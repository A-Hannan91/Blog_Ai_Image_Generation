What is Generative AI

Generative artificial intelligence is artificial intelligence capable of generating text, codes, images, or other media, using generative models. Generative AI models learn the patterns and structure of their input training data and then generate new data that has similar characteristics.

Generative AI refers to a subset of artificial intelligence that focuses on creating models and algorithms capable of generating new content or data that is similar to, or mimics, data that it has been trained on.

AI Image Generation:

Generative AI allows you to transform text and generate realistic images based on the subject, style, setting, or location specified. This makes it possible to generate the required visual material quickly and easily.
These visual materials can then be used for commercial purposes, making AI-generated image creation a useful strategy in fields such as design, advertisement, media marketing, education, etc.

Image Generation works in 3 ways

Art and Creativity

Generative models, particularly Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), have been used to create stunning and imaginative artworks, paintings, and digital designs.

Deepfakes

GANs are employed in deepfake technology, where they generate realistic videos and images by superimposing one person’s face onto another’s.

Style Transfer

Models like neural style transfer can take an image and apply the artistic style of another image to it, creating visually striking combinations.
